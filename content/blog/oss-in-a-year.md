This is going to be a long read but I hope you sit with me here and read through how open source has been with me. From the time I heard about open sourcing, I had loved it, how you can easily build with other developers, build for other developers and gain more hands on techinque with frameworks you loved. But as cool as it was I was afraid to start, I for one suffer from a very huge imposter syndrome and never had much confidence. It takes me so much prep to say the word Hello, not to talk of Hi, this is my PR can you review, I panic before raising an issue in repos that have issues simply because I feareed being seen as a menace or something 😄. That happens a lot over my years of software engineering. But things are much different now, I still feel that low confidence but most times I just go for it, and what could go wrong? I get corrected and learn from the mistake if I make one.

My year fully open source

I started a bit with hacktoberfest and was working through some issues but didn't get to complete it, I'm a student and combining open source work with a full time role wasn't very pleasant experience, burnt out happens so fast.

But I go through 4 oout of 5 PRs, so my firsr hacktoberfest was not much of the success I wanted. But I did make some friends from there Johnkat Mj and a couple of other projects I tried to be in talks with the maintainer.

Moving on, contributing to Open source was just another wish, I want to, I wish to, but couldn't get the very much project I could contribute to. Random ones here and there, but not the ones I was looking for, I was looking for commitment to the project like oh hey, that project had Lope's name to it was my very goal with open source 😄. I don't talk but I wanted my effort and experience to do the talk for me very much.

Fast forward to August 2025, I was rounding off my IT program in computer networking, a 6 month compulsory school program I got an email from Sage (Segun Adebayo) from chakra ui saying he's looking for devs to join chakra ui to help build the next versions of it. I was so elated, I had used chakra ui in early days of my start with IQ.wiki.
